The Animal Farm, written by George Orwell is an allegorical novel that reflects events leading up to the Russian Revolution of 1917 and then on into the Stalinist era of the Soviet Union.

In the map, the ellipse in the centre represents the animal farm.I have placed it in the city of Saint Petersburg where the Russian Revolution began. Click on it to see the picture.

The events are mapped along the boundary to pictorially represent the paw of an animal.To me, it represents the footsteps of change.

Traverse the map from the left most landmark and follow the path to finally reach the animal farm(the ellipse centre)

To view the enlarged images, click on them.
